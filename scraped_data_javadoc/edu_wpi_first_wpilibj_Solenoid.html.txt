Solenoid class for running high voltage Digital Output on a pneumatics module.
The Solenoid class is typically used for pneumatic solenoids, but could be used for any device
 within the current spec of the module.

setPulseDuration
public
void
setPulseDuration
(double durationSeconds)
Set the pulse duration in the pneumatics module. This is used in conjunction with the
 startPulse method to allow the pneumatics module to control the timing of a pulse.
On the PCM, the timing can be controlled in 0.01 second increments, with a maximum of 2.55
 seconds.
On the PH, the timing can be controlled in 0.001 second increments, with a maximum of 65.534
 seconds.
Parameters:
durationSeconds
- The duration of the pulse in seconds.
