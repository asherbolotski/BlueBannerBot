Get the output power behavior when the channel is disabled.
When the channel is enabled [ServoChannel::SetEnabled(true)], the output power to the servo
 follows the channel's power setting [ServoChannel::SetPowered()].
When the channel is disabled [ServoChannel::SetEnabled(false)], the output power to the
 servo follows the channel's disableBehavior.
Returns:
The channel's disable behavior
