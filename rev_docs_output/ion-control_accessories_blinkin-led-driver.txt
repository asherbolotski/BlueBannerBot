The
Blinkin LED Driver (REV-11-1105)
is a compact, all-in-one solution that can control LEDs in a stand-alone mode with just a 12V power source or in a dynamic mode, changing patterns by supplying a standard servo-style PWM signal.

Blinkin LED Driver
